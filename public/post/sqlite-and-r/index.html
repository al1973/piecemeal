SQLite and R

May 4, 2018 4 min read

First, create an in-memory RSQLite database:

library(RSQLite)
library(DBI)
con <- dbConnect(SQLite(), ":memory:")

List its tables (0 at the moment):

dbListTables(con)
## character(0)

Insert a new table – use the mtcars dataset:

dbWriteTable(con, "mtcars", mtcars)

Now, list the tables:

dbListTables(con)
## [1] "mtcars"

List the fields in the table:

dbListFields(con, "mtcars")
##  [1] "mpg"  "cyl"  "disp" "hp"   "drat" "wt"   "qsec" "vs"   "am"   "gear"
## [11] "carb"
